Chocolate Cake

"This Chocolate cake recipe is vert easy and tasty, which children will definately enjoy and can prepare themselves."



DifficultyPreparationCookingTotal Time
Easy10 Min25 Min35 Min

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate : 200g

Sugar : 150g

Butter : 200g

Egg : 4

Flour : 80g

Instructions

1

Melth the chocolate and butter together.

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour.

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough.

4

Bake in the owen and do this do..
